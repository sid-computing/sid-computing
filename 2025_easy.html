Q1: Find the number of positive integers n less than or equal to 100, which are divisible by 3 but are not divisible by 2.

Solution: 33 numbers between 1 and 100 are divisible by 3. 16 numbers between 1 and 100 are divisible by 6. So 17 numbers are divisible by 3 but not by 6, i.e., not divisible by 2.
